MODIFIED ATMOSPHERE PACKAGING

GAS MIXER

Solution for MAP gas mix

2 / 3 gas digital mixers is specially designed for modified atmosphere packaging applications, where the gases N2, CO2 and O2 are used in either 2 or 3 gas combinations.

Elixir technologies digital gasmixers can be integrated with any packaging machine, either buffer tank or stand alone mode.

Depending upon the Gas mixer gives upto 150 l / min gas flow rate
